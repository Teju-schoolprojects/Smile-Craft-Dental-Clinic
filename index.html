Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Smile Craft accept insurance?

Yes, we coordinate directly with all major PPO dental insurances. Our office processes the claim paper files automatically, reducing your out-of-pocket stress.

What are biological/biocompatible dental materials?

We avoid BPA composites, nickel, mercury, and other materials that induce metal allergies. We prioritize biological zirconia, ceramic fillers, and ozone sterilization therapies.

What if I have severe dentist anxiety?

Our treatment rooms are designed as medical spas with noise-canceling headphones, custom aromatherapy, and optional sleep/laughing sedation options to guarantee ease.
